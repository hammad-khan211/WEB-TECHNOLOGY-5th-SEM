ABES Engineering College is a private institution located in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, affiliated with Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU) and accredited with an 'A' Grade by NAAC. The college offers a variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, primarily in engineering, including B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA, and MBA. It is known for its modern infrastructure, including well-equipped labs and classrooms, though some students note the campus size is relatively compact. The college has consistently been ranked in the NIRF ranking for engineering, typically placing in the 201-300 band over the last few years. According to recent reports, ABES Engineering College has a decent placement record, with top recruiters visiting the campus and a reported high placement percentage. While the college is known for a strict attendance and uniform policy, it also has a number of student clubs and activities.
